Petition for 100% Renewable and Carbon Neutral Energy
Vanderbilt University



Dear Chancellor Zeppos,

America’s colleges and universities are significant users of energy and influential institutions in their communities.


Action by Vanderbilt to move towards a clean, renewable future will set an example for the rest of the Nashville Community, the Southeast, and the nation. We are encouraged by the recent commitment and action taken to drastically reduce Vanderbilt’s carbon emissions. To meet goals of carbon reduction, we urge a strong commitment to a steady transition away from fossil fuels.


We, the students, faculty, staff, administration, and alumni of Vanderbilt University, urge you to commit our campus to:


• power all electricity with clean, renewable energy by no later than 2030

• commit to carbon neutral operations by no later than 2050

• Take leadership in educating and encouraging a transition to renewable energy in the region and among our peer institutions
