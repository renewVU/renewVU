Honor's Thesis

For my honor's thesis, I extracted over 60,000 real estate transactions from the Nashville Property Assesor's office to make a comprehensive record of all sales in Davidson County between 2013 and 2016. I then matched that data to US Census and American Community Survey Tract Demographic information to investigate gentrification trends. I was ultimately able to make conclusions about which demographics and communities are most vulnerable.
